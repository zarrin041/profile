SCREENING OF L– ASPARAGINASE PRODUCING BACTERIA FROM SOIL SAMPLES

The objective of the research is to isolate and identify the l-asparagine bacteria, determine the height l-asparagine activities strine and produce the enzyme with better activity. L-asparaginase is a potential anti-carcinogenic enzyme. Two different soil samples of Banasree lack regions were screened for potential producers of L-asparaginase using modified czapek dox’s agar containing L-asparagine. The strain isolated from soil samples (BSH-3) showed the maximum activity.The enzyme production was carried out by submerged fermentation. The maximum enzyme activity was1394.4[u/ml] is shown by 97 hrs culture at 37° C.
